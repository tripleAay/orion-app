Morningstar

About Our Integration

Morningstar® ByAllAccountsSM provides financial account aggregation from over 15,000 different financial institutions to Orion advisors and their clients. Advisors can collect a 360 degree view of their clients’ assets, both held direct and held away. About Integration Partner

Morningstar® ByAllAccountsSM is an aggregation provider that focuses exclusively on investors, advisors, and the enterprises who support them. With over 22 years of experience, Morningstar® ByAllAccountsSM understands what it takes to deliver high-quality, interoperable account data for the complex needs of the wealth management industry. They are a trusted partner of the industry’s leading portfolio management platforms.

Salesforce

About Our Integration

Orion and Salesforce utilize a two-way sync, so your data is accurate in both apps, all the time. When a change is made in one system, it’s updated and reflected in the other. Maintain portfolio data, open new accounts, and see all your portfolio data down to the individual transactions inside Salesforce, or utilize single sign-on access points within Salesforce to open the appropriate client and action in Orion.

About Integration Partner

Salesforce, the global CRM leader, empowers companies to connect with their customers in a whole new way.
